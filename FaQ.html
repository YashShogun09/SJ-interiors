Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
At SJ Interiors, we provide a wide range of interior design services, including space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, and project management.
How does the design process work?
Our design process begins with a consultation to understand your vision, followed by conceptual planning and design proposals. Once approved, we handle the execution to bring your dream space to life.
Do you handle renovations and remodels?
Yes, we specialize in both renovations and remodels, working with trusted contractors to ensure quality and timely completion of your project.
How much do interior design services cost?
Our pricing varies based on the scope of the project. We offer a transparent pricing model and provide a detailed estimate after an initial consultation.
Can you work with my existing furniture?
Absolutely! We can incorporate your existing furniture and decor into the new design, ensuring it complements the new aesthetic
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote planning or live outside of our immediate service area.
How long does a project typically take?
Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and size of the project. We provide an estimated timeline after our initial consultation.
