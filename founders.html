Sai Kumar Kanuri: CEO & Founder

As a specialist in interior architecture and design, Sai Kumar Kanuri is known for blending timeless elegance with modern innovation. His approach to design emphasizes originality and sophistication, ensuring that every space is visually captivating. With a keen eye for detail, Kanuri leads the creative side of the studio, developing personalized design concepts that reflect the unique style and personality of each client.

Rani Swapna Reddy: Co-Founder

Complementing Kanuri's creative expertise, Rani Swapna Reddy is a master of project management. She ensures that each project is executed with precision and efficiency, overseeing the practical aspects of design, from budgeting to timelines. Rani's deep commitment to client satisfaction and her ability to translate creative concepts into actionable plans make her an invaluable part of the team. She ensures that projects are completed on time, on budget, and to the highest standards of quality.



Together, Sai Kumar Kanuri and Rani Swapna Reddy form a dynamic partnership that drives SJ Interiors DnD. Their combined expertise ensures that every project, regardless of scale, is approached with both artistic flair and operational efficiency.
