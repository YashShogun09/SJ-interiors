Designs and Decors

SJ Interiors DnD is a premier interior design firm catering to home and institutional clients, driven by a profound commitment to sustainability and excellence.

Interior Design Styles

#1
Modern Farmhouse
The Modern Farmhouse style combines rustic charm with contemporary elegance. Reclaimed wood, barn doors, and open shelving are complemented by sleek lines and neutral palettes. This aesthetic creates a cozy, warm atmosphere while maintaining a clean, modern feel. It's perfect for those who want a blend of tradition and innovation.
#2
House Of Patterns
Dive into a world of vibrant designs with the House of Patterns style. Intricate motifs, geometric shapes, and bold colors dominate this look. Textiles like kilim rugs, patterned cushions, and detailed wallpapers create an energetic and expressive space. It’s the perfect style for anyone who loves to make a statement.
#3
The Minimalist
Embrace simplicity with The Minimalist style, where every element has a purpose. Featuring clean lines, neutral tones, and open spaces, this style promotes tranquility and order. Functional furniture and limited decor keep the focus on essentials, creating a serene and clutter-free environment.
#4
The Gatsby Gala
Experience the grandeur of the roaring twenties with The Gatsby Gala. Think metallic finishes, bold geometric patterns, and luxurious materials like velvet and marble. This Art Deco-inspired style is perfect for those who love sophistication and drama, creating a space that’s both opulent and timeless.
#5
Staycation
Turn your home into a luxurious retreat with the Staycation style. Soft furnishings, earthy tones, and plenty of greenery create a relaxed, inviting vibe. This look prioritizes comfort and leisure, with spa-inspired bathrooms, cozy reading nooks, and warm lighting that make every day feel like a holiday.
#6
Under The Arch
The Under The Arch style embraces architectural curves and rounded shapes for a soft, elegant look. Arched doorways, curved furniture, and circular decor elements create a harmonious flow throughout the space. Paired with neutral tones and textured walls, it exudes timeless sophistication.
#7
Pastel Dream
Soft, dreamy, and playful, the Pastel Dream style uses delicate colors like blush pink, mint green, and lavender. Combined with airy fabrics, subtle patterns, and whimsical decor, this design creates a serene and uplifting environment that’s both stylish and cozy.
#8
In The Grey
Sophisticated and versatile, the In The Grey style revolves around shades of grey. This neutral palette is balanced with contrasting textures and materials like concrete, metal, and soft fabrics. The result is a modern, elegant space with a calming and understated charm.
#9
Sculpted Harmony
Sculpted Harmony is a blend of art and functionality. Featuring statement furniture pieces, sculptural lighting, and unique decor, this style transforms a home into an artistic haven. Neutral tones and natural materials enhance the sophisticated aesthetic, creating a space that’s as beautiful as it is practical.
#10
Indian Modernist
Combining traditional Indian elements with modern design, the Indian Modernist style showcases vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and handcrafted decor. Paired with clean lines and contemporary furniture, it celebrates India’s rich heritage while embracing a global outlook.
#11
Urban Oasis
Urban Oasis creates a green retreat within the city. Incorporating indoor plants, natural materials, and calming colors, this style brings nature indoors. Paired with modern furniture and functional layouts, it’s a perfect escape from urban hustle while maintaining contemporary aesthetics.
#12
Classic Contemporary
Blending timeless elegance with modern sophistication, Classic Contemporary uses neutral palettes, luxurious materials, and understated decor. Featuring elegant furniture and harmonious layouts, this style offers a perfect balance between tradition and innovation.
